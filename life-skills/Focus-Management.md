# Focus Management Answers

### Question 1  
Deep Work means focusing on one important task without any distractions.  
It is when your mind is fully involved in the work and you use your full brain power.  
This helps you finish better work in less time.

---

### Question 2  
How to do deep work properly (according to the author):

- Work in a place with no distractions  
- Fix a time for deep work every day  
- Do only one important task during that time  
- Keep phone and social media away  
- Take breaks to relax the mind  
- Make deep work a daily habit

### Question 4  
Dangers of social media:

- Reduces focus and attention  
- Makes us compare our life with others  
- Can create stress and anxiety  
- Wastes a lot of time  
- Affects sleep  
- Makes deep work harder
