# Listening and Active Communication

## 1. Active Listening

### Answer to Question 1
- Avoid getting distracted by your own thought.
- Focus on speaker and topics.
- Do not interrupt
- SHow them you are listning with body language.
- Take note of important conversation
- Ask clarifying questions

## 2. Reflective Listening

### Answer to Question 2
- Listen to words and feelings
- Repeat the message in simple words
- Check if I understood correctly
- Focus on the emotion behind the message
- Encourage the speaker to continue
- Avoid judging or giving advice too early

## 3. Reflection

### Answer to Question 3
- I get distracted easily
- I think about my reply too early
- Background noise affects me
- I lose focus when stressed

### Answer to Question 4
- Keep my phone away
- Ask clarifying questions
- Reflect back what the speaker said
- Avoid interrupting
- Stay mentally present

## 4. Types of Communication

### Answer to Question 5
- When I want to avoid conflict
- When I feel less confident
- When the other person is dominating

### Answer to Question 6
- When I am frustrated
- When I feel ignored
- When someone is rude to me

### Answer to Question 7
- When I am upset but don’t say it directly
- When I use sarcasm
- When I stay silent instead of expressing feelings

### Answer to Question 8
- Use clear and direct words
- Use “I” statements
- Stay calm and respectful
- Set boundaries politely
- Say no in a simple and honest way
- Express needs without anger or fear
