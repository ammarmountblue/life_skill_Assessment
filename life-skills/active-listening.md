# Listening and Active Communication

## 1. Active Listening

### Answer to Question 1
- I give full attention
- I don’t interrupt
- I ask questions when I don’t understand
- I say back what I understood
- I notice the person’s feelings and tone
- I avoid distractions like phone or noise
- I show I’m listening by nodding

## 2. Reflective Listening

### Answer to Question 2
- I listen to both words and feelings
- I repeat the message in simple words
- I check if I understood correctly
- I focus on the emotion behind the message
- I encourage the person to keep talking
- I don’t judge or give advice too early

## 3. Reflection

### Answer to Question 3
- I get distracted easily
- I think about my reply too soon
- Background noise distracts me
- I lose focus when I am stressed

### Answer to Question 4
- I will keep my phone away
- I will ask questions to understand better
- I will repeat what the speaker said
- I will not interrupt
- I will try to stay fully present

## 4. Types of Communication

### Answer to Question 5 (Passive)
- When I want to avoid a fight
- When I feel less confident
- When the other person talks too much

### Answer to Question 6 (Aggressive)
- When I am frustrated
- When I feel ignored
- When someone is rude to me

### Answer to Question 7 (Passive-Aggressive)
- When I am upset but don’t say it directly
- When I use sarcasm
- When I stay silent instead of expressing my feelings

### Answer to Question 8 (Assertive)
- I use clear and simple words
- I use “I” statements
- I stay calm and respectful
- I set boundaries politely
- I say no clearly and honestly
- I express my needs without anger or fear
