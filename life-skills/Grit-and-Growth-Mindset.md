# Grit and Growth Mindset Answers


## **1. Grit Video Summary**

The video says that success comes from sticking with your goals for a long time and not giving up. Hard work and patience matter more than talent.


## **2. Growth Mindset Video Summary**

The video explains that people can improve their abilities if they keep learning and practicing. Your brain grows when you challenge yourself instead of saying “I can’t do this”.


## **3. Internal Locus of Control**

**What is it?**  
Internal Locus of Control means believing that "my life depends on my actions, not luck or others."

**Key point:**  
You stay motivated when you feel you are in charge of your life. Taking responsibility gives you power to change things.


## **4. Key Points to Build Growth Mindset**

- Accept challenges  
- Learn from mistakes  
- Keep trying  
- Believe you can improve  
- Take feedback  
- Celebrate effort, not only results  
- Stay curious  
- Practice daily


## **5. My Ideas to Take Action and Build Growth Mindset**

- Try new things even if they feel hard  
- When I fail, I will learn instead of feeling sad  
- I will ask questions and take feedback  
- I will practice regularly instead of waiting for motivation  
- I will surround myself with positive people  
- I will celebrate small improvements  
- I will remind myself that progress takes time
